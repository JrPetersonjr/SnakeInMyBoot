ROOTSIGNAL FIX USB - DO THIS (BOOT LOOP)

1) Turn computer off.
2) Plug in USB.
3) Turn computer on and open Boot Menu (Dell/Lenovo/Acer F12, HP F9).
4) Choose USB Device.
5) On blue Windows screen: Repair your computer -> Troubleshoot -> Command Prompt.
6) Run one of these:
   G:\startfix.bat
   G:\RUN-ROOTFIX.cmd
   G:\RootFix\fixme.bat
   (If not G:, use your USB letter.)
7) In the green menu choose:
   1  Recommended Auto Repair
8) Wait for it to finish, then restart.

WINDOWS IS BOOTING NORMALLY? USE THIS INSTEAD:
- Open USB drive in File Explorer.
- Double-click: RootFix\RUN-IN-WINDOWS.cmd
- Choose option 1 (Prep update safely).
