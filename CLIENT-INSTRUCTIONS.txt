ROOTSIGNAL FIX USB - DO THIS

1) Turn the computer off.
2) Plug in the USB stick.
3) Turn the computer on.
4) Tap the boot menu key:
   Dell: F12
   HP: F9
   Lenovo: F12
   Acer: F12
5) Choose USB Device.
6) On the blue Windows screen: Repair your computer -> Troubleshoot -> Command Prompt
7) In Command Prompt, run this (replace D: with your USB drive letter if needed):
   D:\RootFix\fixme.bat
8) In the green terminal menu, choose:
   1 (Recommended: Auto repair)
9) Wait for completion, then restart.

If internet is available and your technician asks, choose option 2.
