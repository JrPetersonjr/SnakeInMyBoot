ROOTSIGNAL FIX USB - DO THIS

1) Turn the computer off.
2) Plug in the USB stick.
3) Turn the computer on.
4) Tap the key that opens the boot menu:
   Dell: F12
   HP: F9
   Lenovo: F12
   Acer: F12
5) Choose USB Device.
6) When the blue Windows screen appears, click:
   Repair your computer -> Troubleshoot -> Command Prompt
7) Type and press Enter:
   X:\RootFix\fixme.bat
8) Wait until it says Done.
9) Close the window and restart the computer.
